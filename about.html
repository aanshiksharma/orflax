Who are we?

At Orflax India Pvt. Ltd., we’re not just manufacturers—we’re innovators. As one of the leading names in the electrical wires and cables industry, Orflax is committed to pushing boundaries and setting new standards of excellence. From our cutting-edge research and development to the precision of our manufacturing, every cable and wire we produce reflects our uncompromising dedication to quality and performance.
